Wiki & Frequently Asked Questions

What is this?

Parcel Tycoon is an idle/management game inspired by Pocket Planes ‚Äî but for trucks. You build a delivery company: buy trucks, take contracts, run routes, research upgrades, expand storage with warehouses, and eventually Prestige to earn permanent multipliers.

Core Concepts

  • Money: Earned from deliveries and small passive income via HQ Ops research.
  • Contracts: Click Generate Contracts, then Accept to reserve storage. Dispatch trucks to complete them.
  • Trucks: Each has capacity, fuel, speed, and efficiency. Refuel to keep them moving.
  • Routes: Procedurally generated between cities. Distance impacts delivery time and fuel usage.
  • Warehouses: Increase storage capacity for larger contracts.
  • Research: Permanent upgrades like better engines, more capacity, HQ operations, fuel hedging, and fleet tier.
  • Prestige (Logos): Reset your company to gain Logos (permanent multiplier). Logos are based on lifetime deliveries.
  • Offline Progress: Earn a capped amount while away.
  • Saving: Auto-saves periodically. You can also save/load or export/import JSON manually.

How do I progress?

  1. Buy a truck and generate contracts.
  2. Accept a contract and dispatch your truck (it will auto-load up to its capacity).
  3. Use money to build warehouses (bigger storage) and buy more trucks.
  4. Invest in research to speed up, carry more, and stabilize fuel costs.
  5. Prestige when progress slows ‚Äî multiply future earnings!

Prestige Details

You gain Logos according to floor(sqrt(lifetimeDeliveries / 25)). Each Logo adds +5% to your money and passive income multiplier. Prestige resets trucks, contracts, warehouses, and research, but keeps Logos.

Tips & Tricks

  • Refuel before long routes; stranded trucks pause progress.
  • HQ Ops increases contract slots and passive income, which helps early idling.
  • Fuel Hedging reduces randomness in fuel price swings.
  • Fleet Tier makes newly bought trucks stronger out of the box.
